and where each one goes.

00 · The principle

One rule: features become cards, not screens.

~/gui is a 25-section operations console with a sidebar, tabs, tables, and toolbars. That density is exactly what we are moving away from. Every feature in this deck lands in one of three surfaces, and nothing earns a permanent nav slot.

SURFACE 01

Inline card

The default. The agent answers with a rich card right in the thread where you asked. Runs, diffs, gates, schedules, facts, all render as a card in the assistant lane.

Implement · auth refactor
plan ✓ · editing files… · tests pending
SURFACE 02

One focused canvas

For things you study, the tree, the graph, the diff, the timeline, a single canvas slides in beside a slim chat rail. One at a time. Drag the seam, share the URL.

SURFACE 03

Corner toast

Ambient only. A background run, a waiting approval, a finished job. It nudges from the corner and gets out of the way. Click to jump back to its card.

15 · Putting it together

Build the live-run core first; the rest are cards.

Every feature lands on one of three surfaces and is reachable by a slash command or by the agent surfacing it. Suggested order: the run lifecycle (P0), then the study tools (P1), then the on-demand cards (P2).
